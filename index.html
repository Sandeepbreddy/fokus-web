Frequently Asked Questions

How does Fokus block websites?

Fokus intercepts requests to distracting websites and redirects you to a motivational blocked page, keeping you on track with your goals.

Can I temporarily disable blocking?

Yes! You can pause blocking for 5, 15, or 30 minutes, or disable it completely when you need full access to the internet.

What are GitHub blocklists?

Community-maintained lists of thousands of domains (ads, trackers, adult content, etc.) that you can import with one click instead of adding sites manually.

Does it work in Incognito mode?

Yes, if you enable the extension in Incognito mode through Chrome's extension settings, Fokus will work there too.
